Who's behind Xenon Apps?

At our heart we're a group of problem solvers. Life always has problems, and for some of them technology is the right solution. We are providers of those solutions. Experienced in numerous languages, platforms, and frameworks; each member of the Xenon team brings a unique perspective and set of skills to each problem solved.

Most of us here at Xenon Apps are based from Burlington VT, USA. But we have a great designer from Somersworth, NH and have collaborated with another who lives in New Jersey on our Green Up VT application. If you want to get to know us, check out some of our profiles below.
