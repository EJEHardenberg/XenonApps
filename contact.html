Contacting our team

General Correspondence

If you'd like to get in touch with just one of us: you can use our convention of firstname@xenonapps.com.

Contributing

If you'd like to contribute to one of the projects we work on, visit its home page and fork the project, open an issue, get in touch and code. We love to code, and if you do too then we'll probably get along.

Consultations

We're just a group of developers; if you've got an outstanding idea you'd like to implement or discuss. Get in touch with us and we're more than happy to explore what open-sourcing your project could do for you.
